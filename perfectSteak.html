Perfect Steak Recipe

Description

Flat iron steak is a tender, flavorful cut of meat that is perfect for grilling. This recipe is easy to make and is sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 pounds flat iron steak
