Cheesy Ramen Noodles

Description

These cheesy ramen noodles are a quick and easy meal that is perfect for busy weeknights. This recipe is easy to make and is sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 (3 ounce) package ramen noodles
  • 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup frozen peas
  • 1/4 cup chopped cooked ham

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil. Add ramen noodles and cook for 3 minutes, or until tender. Drain and return to the pot.
  2. Stir in the cheese, milk, peas, and ham. Cook and stir over medium heat until the cheese is melted and the peas are heated through.
